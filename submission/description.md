# EVM: The Machine

**Step inside the World Computer.** A first-person journey through six chambers of Ethereum history, built solo in 48 hours for Gamedev.js Jam 2026.

Walk through Genesis. Escape The DAO's reentrant hall of mirrors. Convert PoW miners into PoS validators at The Merge. Dodge gas-price storms. Jump through rollup layers. Reach Vitalik's Core. Every chamber you finish is recorded on Sepolia, and clearing all six mints you a one-of-a-kind Journey NFT.

The chain is your souvenir — not a login gate. The game is fully playable offline.

## Controls
- **WASD** move · **Mouse** look · **E** interact · **Shift** sprint · **Space** jump · **Left click** fire · **Esc** quit to menu

## Features
- 6 unique chambers, each with a distinct mechanic
- Procedurally generated visuals — no imported models or textures
- Original synth-wave BGM composed at runtime with Tone.js
- Optional on-chain progress tracking (Sepolia testnet, free)
- Journey NFT mint on completion

## Challenges entered
- **Open Source** — full source on GitHub with inline docs
- **Ethereum** — meaningful on-chain journey record, not a bolted-on login
- **Wavedash** — deployed identically to Wavedash

## Stack
Three.js · Vite · viem · Tone.js · Foundry · Solidity
