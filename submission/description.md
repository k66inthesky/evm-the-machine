# EVM: The Machine

**Step inside the World Computer.** A first-person narrative game through eight moments of Ethereum history, built solo in 48 hours for Gamedev.js Jam 2026 (theme: *Machines*).

You walk into a 2013 Hong Kong dorm room, type a reply on a CRT, then step into an attic full of equations and pick the opening sentence of the Ethereum whitepaper. Then it's the Zug spaceship-house, the 42-day crowdsale, the reentrant DAO, the fork, the DeFi bloom, and finally the last PoW server room on Merge night.

Every chamber asks one small first-person decision. The machine silently records what kind of person each decision implies. After the last chapter, a mirror shows your archetype — Visionary, Engineer, Capitalist, Governor, Rebel, Speculator, Builder, or Witness.

The chain is your souvenir, not a login gate. The game is fully playable offline.

## Controls
- **WASD** move · **Mouse** look · **E** interact · **1/2/3/4** choose · **Esc / Q / click QUIT** to menu

## Features
- Eight first-person chapters, each its own diegetic 3D scene
- A hidden 8-dim archetype tracker that reveals who you are at the finale
- Procedurally generated visuals — no imported models or textures, every whiteboard equation drawn at runtime
- Original synthwave BGM composed live in Tone.js — no audio assets shipped
- Optional on-chain progress tracking (Sepolia testnet, free)
- `mintJourney` souvenir NFT once all eight chapters are complete

## Challenges entered
- **Open Source** — full source on GitHub, every file headed with a what-and-why comment
- **Ethereum** — meaningful on-chain journey record, not a bolted-on login
- **Wavedash** — deployed identically to Wavedash
